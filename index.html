Murphy Brown The Dog

By Kasey Knudsen

Murphy is your basic labradoodle--great on some days, and a terrorist on other days. He was a famous sock thief for the first 2 years of his life until he decided to move on to smaller and better things, like ear plugs and tampons. He loves to snuggle and shake hands, hump his bed and bark at the mail man, eat fresh pasta and run away from me when I try to clean his ears, and most of all, he loves to stand between us and the kitchen counter when we're cooking, just in case any crumbs fall on the floor. He was born on March 17th, 2017, St Patrick's day. We named him Murphy Brown after both the beloved sitcom, and the delectable Irish stout.

Bath Time

It takes several cookies and coaxing to get Murphy into the bath, but once he's in, he's quite well behaved. Once he's out, he runs around the apartment destroying everything in his path.
